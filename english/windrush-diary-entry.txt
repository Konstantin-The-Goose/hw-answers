Windrush diary entry
I cannot believe it. A simple advert, printed in ink, has suddenly uprooted my entire life. They said Britain needed workers, they said Britain has streets paved with gold. 
Such an amazing opportunity, it was very hard to pass up. Lots of people are going, ships are setting off, buses roaring past. It feels like a new beginning. 
I decided to go. My ship was the empire windrush, we sailed for a few days. The journey was a bit brutal, but I thought it was worth it at the time. But the second I came, what did I get? 
Rain, cold. I tried to go somewhere. I got stopped immediately. I explained I was looking for shelter. They thought I was breaking in.
Maybe it wasn't so good after all? Why would I even come to this country?
